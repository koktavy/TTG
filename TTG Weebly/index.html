What is TTG?

 We are a club designed to help NYU Tisch students find and collaborate with artists who can help bring their projects to life. From actors to photographers to writers, directors, and producers, the Tisch Talent Guild can help you find the artists to turn your vision into a reality.

Submit.

Submit headshots, resumes, projects, scripts, reels, inquiries, and more!

Discover.

Find talent, projects, creatives, production, and more!

Collaborate.

Attend TTG events to expand your artistic network and plan for future projects.
